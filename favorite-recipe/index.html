Strawberry Cheesecake Cookies

>Wonderfully soft strawberry cookies with goey cream cheese fillings.

(No strawberries required)

Ingredients

  • Batter Ingredients
    • 1 cup butter
    • 1/4 cup of packed brown sugar (light or dark)
    • 1 cup white granulated sugar
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
    • 1/2 tsp strawberry extract
    • 1 tsp red/pink food coloring
    • 2 1/2 cups of All-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1/2 tsp baking powder
    • 1/4 tsp of salt (optional if you used salted butter)
  • Cream Cheese Filling
    • 1 cup cream cheese
    • 2/3 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Topping
    • Approximately 1 cup granulated/white sugar (for coating the cookies)
